Main Course

Main Course offers a variety of food and beverage options for all taste buds. There’s a range of options including pastas, rice dishes, burgers, hot food, roasts, salads, sandwiches, noodles, dumplings and a make-your-own sandwich, salad or wrap section. Our food are prepared freshly daily on-site.

Kebab & Co

Located within the Main Course dining precinct, Kebab & Co offers freshly kebabs made to order. Their kebabs are made from the freshest, highest quality ingredients – the traditional way.

Subway

Located within the Main Course dining precinct, Subway offers customised sandwiches, wraps and salads.

Guzman Y Gomez

Located in the new Union Arcade within the Union Arcade Guzman Y Gomez's menu is built on traditional Mexican flavours and made from only the highest quality ingredients.

On a Roll Bakery

On a Roll Bakery offers mouth-watering breakfast, morning tea and lunch options like bagels, bacon & cheese wraps, croissants, gourmet sandwiches, pastries, pies, donuts, cakes & slices. They are conveniently located in the centre of the UQU complex opposite STA Travel. Cold drinks are also available here.

The Pizza Caffe

Pizza Caffe is Brisbane's hidden gem, located within the University of Queensland on the St Lucia campus. Enjoy one of their 26 gourmet pizzas from our famous A-Z menu, which are inspired by Italian movies and actors in the licensed alfresco-style outdoor area or takeaway. Pizza Caffe also offers lasagne and salads, as well as coffee, alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks. Gluten free pizza bases are also available.

Red Room Bar & Grill

For decades, UQ students have flocked to the Red Room for a feed to accompany their beverage desires. Come to the Red Room with your mates for a drink and grab come hot chips, nachos, steak or the famous Red Room burger. You can chill out on the Red Room deck or play pool. More than just a place to drink, the Red Room is the entertainment hub on campus where you can see anything from Dj’s to trivia, from themed parties to karaoke

The Physiol Eatery and Cafe

Physiol Eatery & Café has everything you need. The café serves coffee, hot chocolate, tea, iced beverages and frappes. Whilst the Eatery offers a large variety of breakfast and lunch options such as a make-your-own sandwich and wrap station, sushi, rice & pasta dishes, roasts meals & sandwiches, burgers, hot dogs, chips, sausage rolls & pies, snacks, ice cream, cakes & slices and cold drinks. There is also a large indoor and outdoor seating area for you to sit, relax and enjoy.

Boost Juice

Located next to Mr Beans in the Union Complex, Boost Juice is a favourite amongst students and staff looking for a liquid lunch, post workout smoothie or fresh juice.
